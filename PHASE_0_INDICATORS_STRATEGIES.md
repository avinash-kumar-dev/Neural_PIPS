# Comprehensive Indicators & Strategies Research

## PART 1: TREND INDICATORS

### ADX (Average Directional Index)
- **Optimal Settings**: Period 14 (standard) or 7-10 for faster signals
- **Threshold**: ADX > 25 confirms trend; ADX > 30 = strong trend
- **Timeframe**: M5 (primary), M15 (confirmation)
- **Strategies**: ADX Filter, +DI/-DI Crossover, ADX + MACD Confluence
- **Win Rate**: ADX > 25 filter improves win rates by 25%+

### EMA (Exponential Moving Average)
- **Scalping Settings**: EMA(9) and EMA(21) on M5
- **Fast Scalping**: EMA(5) and EMA(9) on M1-M5
- **Trend Confirmation**: EMA(20) and EMA(9)
- **Session Filter**: EMA(200) on M15-H1
- **Strategies**: EMA Crossover, EMA Ribbon, 9/21 EMA + RSI

### MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence)
- **Standard**: 12, 26, 9
- **Scalping**: 5, 13, 8 or 5, 35, 5
- **Ultra-fast**: 3, 10, 1
- **Strategies**: Zero-Line Cross, Signal Line Crossover, Histogram Divergence

### Parabolic SAR
- **Scalping Settings**: Step 0.03, Max 0.30
- **Conservative**: Step 0.01, Max 0.10
- **Strategies**: PSAR + EMA(200), PSAR + RSI, Trailing Stop Tool

### Ichimoku Kinko Hyo
- **Scalping Settings**: 6, 13, 26, 26 (faster)
- **Standard**: 9, 26, 52, 26
- **Strategies**: Kumo Breakout, Tenkan/Kijun Cross, Kijun-Sen Pullback

## PART 2: MOMENTUM INDICATORS

### RSI (Relative Strength Index)
- **Standard**: RSI(14) with levels 30/70
- **Scalping**: RSI(7) for faster signals
- **Trend Bands**: RSI(14) with levels 40/60
- **Strategies**: Pullback Entry, Divergence, Centerline Cross

### Stochastic Oscillator
- **Standard**: %K=14, %D=3, Slowing=3
- **Fast Scalping**: %K=5, %D=3, Slowing=3
- **M1 Optimized**: %K=8, %D=3, Slowing=3
- **Strategies**: Stochastic Hook, Dual Stochastic, Cross Confirmation

### CCI (Commodity Channel Index)
- **Standard**: CCI(14) with levels +100/-100
- **Faster**: CCI(7)
- **Strategies**: Zero-Line Cross, Triple Confirmation (RSI + CCI + Stochastic)

## PART 3: VOLATILITY INDICATORS

### Bollinger Bands
- **Standard**: 20-period SMA with 2 standard deviations
- **Squeeze Detection**: Bandwidth contraction below 0.0005
- **Strategies**: Mean Reversion (Bollinger Bounce), Bollinger Squeeze, Double Bollinger Bands

### ATR (Average True Range)
- **Standard**: ATR(14)
- **Scalping**: ATR(7) or ATR(10)
- **Stop Sizing**: 1.2x to 1.8x ATR(14)
- **Strategies**: ATR-Based Stops, Volatility Filter, Position Sizing

### Keltner Channels
- **Scalping**: EMA 15-20, ATR 10, Multiplier 1.5
- **Standard**: EMA 20, ATR 20, Multiplier 2.0
- **Strategies**: Trend-Pullback, Breakout, Squeeze Detection

## PART 5: SMART MONEY CONCEPTS (SMC/ICT)

### Core Concepts
- **Break of Structure (BOS)**: Price breaks swing high/low, confirming trend
- **Change of Character (CHoCH)**: First break against prevailing structure
- **Order Blocks (OB)**: Last opposing candle before impulsive move
- **Fair Value Gaps (FVG)**: Three-candle structure with wick gap
- **Liquidity Sweeps**: False break followed by sharp reversal

### ICT Kill Zones
- **Asian Session**: 8:00 PM - 12:00 AM EST
- **London Kill Zone**: 2:00 AM - 5:00 AM EST
- **New York Kill Zone**: 7:00 AM - 10:00 AM EST

### SMC Scalping Strategy
1. Daily Bias from daily/4H structure
2. Mark Liquidity levels
3. Wait for Kill Zone
4. Liquidity Sweep
5. CHoCH or BOS confirmation
6. Entry at FVG or Order Block
7. Stop beyond sweep high/low
8. Target opposing liquidity pool

**Reported Win Rate**: 55-70% with proper confluence
